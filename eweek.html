National Engineers Week is an annual celebration honoring the contributions of engineers and inspiring the next generation. At Oregon State University, the College of Engineering runs a full week of events connecting students to industry, alumni, and hands-on experiences.

Overview

About the Event

E-Week is the College of Engineering's flagship annual celebration of National Engineers Week at Oregon State University. Over the course of a full week, the College hosts a lineup of workshops, panels, competitions, and community events designed to bring engineering students together and connect them with the broader engineering world.

As VP of Outreach for the COE Student Council, I took on full ownership of E-Week's marketing and design — from building a cohesive branding kit from scratch to coordinating with the events team to shape the week's programming.
