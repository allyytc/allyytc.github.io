Innovation
Jam 2026

Innovation Jam is the Willamette Valley's flagship student innovation and entrepreneurship event, bringing founders, builders, and the regional startup community to Oregon State University for a day of pitches, workshops, and collaboration. For Innovation Jam 2026, Startup Hub was entrusted to organize the event — the first time a student organization has taken the reins from inventOR.

Overview

About the Event

Innovation Jam brings together student founders, builders, and the broader Willamette Valley startup community for a full day of pitches, hands-on workshops, and networking. Teams compete for $1,500 in cash prizes and a shot at advancing to the statewide inventOR competition, where $30,000 in grants and awards are on the line. Mentors and judges are recruited from venture capital, business, and academia to guide teams through the process.

For Innovation Jam 2026, inventOR handed full ownership of the event to Startup Hub at OSU — the entrepreneurship club I co-founded — making it the first time a student organization has taken complete control of one of the Willamette Valley's flagship innovation events. inventOR provided the budget; we handled everything else.

My Role

As part of Startup Hub's core team, I led operations and design for the event — from early planning and budget allocation through running logistics on the day. Beyond keeping the event on track, I was responsible for all merchandise and branded materials that every attendee, volunteer, judge, and mentor walked away with.

Operations

Event Coordination

  • Managed day-of logistics, including registration, check-in, attendee guidance, and swag distribution.
  • Oversaw budget allocation across food, supplies, merchandise, and event materials.
Design

Branding & Merch

  • Designed the full suite of Innovation Jam '26 branded materials — stickers, tote bags, notebooks, award plaques, judge and mentor gift boxes, and name cards for all attendees, volunteers, and speakers.
  • Screen-printed the tote bags by hand, and self laser engraved the award plaques for winning teams.
  • Managed end-to-end production logistics for all merchandise.
Programming

Pitch Sessions

  • Managed presentation logistics, including AV setup and keeping sessions on schedule.
  • Represented Startup Hub throughout the day, connecting with founders, judges, and fellow organizers.
