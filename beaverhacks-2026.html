BeaverHacks
2026

BeaverHacks is Oregon's largest annual hackathon, hosted by Oregon State University and drawing 500+ students from universities across the country. Over 24 hours, teams of up to four tackle sponsor-based challenges across multiple tracks, building projects from scratch in a fast-paced, collaborative environment. The event bridges the gap between university and industry, giving students direct exposure to the problems and people shaping the tech industry.

My Role

Hackathons hold a special place for me — they're one of the few opportunities to carve out dedicated time for a creative project you'd never get around to otherwise.
